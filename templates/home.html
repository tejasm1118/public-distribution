Foolproof Public Distribution System




About:


    PDS is the largest food distribution network in the world. It has a network of more than 4 lakh Fair price shops that distribute commodities to 16 Crore households at a cost of more than Rs.15,000 Crore.

    Of the total loss incurred by the government 39% is distribution leakage loss, 38% is over-allotment loss and 17% is active suspect loss.

    Fool proof public distribution system will control and prevent the losses incurred to the government. Aadhaar cards of a family is linked to Ration card and a smart card will be provided. The biometric data will help in eliminating the ghost cards. Packing and bar-coding of grains will ensure proper distribution and tracing of the grains.The extras will be accounted back to the government.
